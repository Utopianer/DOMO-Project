Hello World!
   06/2018 Domocoin release

Launch of DOMOcoin, a Cryptocurrency based on the X11 algorithm! To ensure a fair distribution people have 1 month to get as many coins as possible with their miners. NO Premine. Furthermore, we have PRE-announced the launch of Domocoin to avoid instamining.
POW-Phase is over!
   07/2018 Domocoin SWAP (PIVX)

As planned, we are moving to a POS solution! Domocoin is now a custom PivX clone! Coin swap have been initiated and people have 4 months to swap their coins to the new Blockchain!
Updates, Updates everywhere!
   12/2018 More changes - New Blockchain!

After our successful logo competition, we decided to make a fresh start with lots of new features! In addition to our new logo, we added a developers shares reward in the block reward structure. 5% of all block rewards go to our development fund. Furthermore the zerocoin protocoll has been activated, you can send 100% anonymous transactions now!
