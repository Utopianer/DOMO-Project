Domocoin Features

With simplicity and fairness in mind!

Fair Launch

Domocoin launched with fairness in mind. Pre-Announcement + NO PREMINE + A limited POW-Phase for distribution

Community oriented

Domocoin relys on the Community to develop the Currency. No fake Roadmaps or Announcements. Contributions are rewarded in Domocoins.

Proof of Stake Masternodes

Domocoin forked from X11/Dash to full Proof of Stake/PIVX. Masternodes receive 0.9 DOMO per Block, 0.1 Stakingreward!

Limited

There are not many Domocoins in circulation. In order to generate new Coins, 1000 Coins need to be locked up in a Masternode. Just leaving the wallet open also generates Coins!
